âº Regarding detaching your Time Machine drive - there are no significant
  negatives beyond the temporary loss of backup capability:

  Short-term impact:
  - Time Machine backups will pause until you reconnect the drive
  - You'll see system notifications about the backup drive being
  disconnected
  - No immediate data risk - your existing backups remain safe on the drive

  Long-term impact:
  - As long as you reconnect it regularly (weekly/bi-weekly), there's no
  issue
  - Time Machine will resume backing up from where it left off when
  reconnected
  - The main risk is only if something happens to your Mac while the drive
  is disconnected and you haven't backed up recently

  Best practice: Just reconnect it periodically (once a week or so) to keep
  your backups current. It's perfectly safe to disconnect between sessions.
