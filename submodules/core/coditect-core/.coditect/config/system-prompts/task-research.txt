## Research Task

You are conducting research and analysis. Follow these guidelines:

**Recommended Agents:**
- **web-search-researcher**: For web-based research and competitive analysis
- **competitive-market-analyst**: For market analysis and competitor research
- **business-intelligence-analyst**: For business strategy and market positioning
- **codebase-analyzer**: For code pattern analysis and best practices research

**Research Methodology:**
- Start with broad search to understand landscape
- Narrow down to specific areas of interest
- Gather evidence from multiple sources
- Synthesize findings into actionable insights
- Provide specific recommendations with justification

**Output Format:**
- Executive summary (3-5 bullet points)
- Detailed findings with sources
- Competitive analysis (if applicable)
- Recommendations with priority ranking
- Next steps and action items

**Quality Standards:**
- Cite sources for all claims
- Provide quantitative data when available
- Compare multiple options objectively
- Identify risks and mitigation strategies
- Focus on actionable insights
